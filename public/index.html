WHAT WE DO.

Simply put, we are a production studio that has a passion for creating artistic content. A small business, comprised of just five creative brains, all with our own strengths. We offer a variety of services, ranging from creating music videos, to social media promotional content. We also have the ability to completely rebuild your online presence for your business, starting with all new advertising content, up to creating landing pages/websites for you to use for marketing in pair with the new content.

SUPPORTING THE CREATIVE COMMUNITY.

We will always try our best to accomodate any business that approaches us for projects, but we do work with indiviuals as well. Our studio strives to support and uplift any creative voices. Comedians, music artists, writers, and performers alike are all welcome within our studio.

SOME OF THE CREATIVE SERVICES WE OFFER INCLUDE

Creative Projects

If you have a great idea to showcase your talentes, but lack the tools or the people-power to get it off the ground, we are here to help you every step of the way. We strive to bring that hilarious sketch or captivating short film from your mind, to an audience that will love it.

Social Media Marketing

From short cinematic promotional reels, product photos, to full length advertisements, our studio works hard to create eye catching content that works with multiple social media platforms. We have a deep understanding of algorithims, as well as meeting licensing requirements for platforms in order to ensure your advertisement is seen.

Live Event Coverage

Whether you need a single-camera setup, or a multi-person crew, our team is experienced in a vast amount of event and light work. Ranging from comedy special tapings to burlesque shows, we will commit to giving you the best quality videos of your entire event.

Music Videos

Whether you are an established artist or up and coming, we want to help you create a music video that truly expresses who you are artistically. A music video is something that should showcase your vision and highlight your talents, which is something we as a studio specialize in.
